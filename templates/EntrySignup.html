ABOUT US

Our mission is to empower individuals with knowledge and support to overcome eating disorders. Our app is designed for users of all ages, featuring a comprehensive diagnosis system with both Short and Long Screening methods. To help manage urges to overeat, we offer a Distraction tab filled with engaging activities. Additionally, our chatbot provides personalized emotional support. Users earn points through participation, which can be used to acquire attractive cards as a reward for their progress.
